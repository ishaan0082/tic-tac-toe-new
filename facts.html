Facts About Tic Tac Toe

  • Tic Tac Toe, also known as Noughts and Crosses, is a classic paper-and-pencil game.
  • It is usually played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Two players take turns marking an empty cell with their respective symbols, typically 'X' and 'O'.
  • The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins.
  • If all the cells are filled and no player has three marks in a row, the game is a draw.
  • Tic Tac Toe is often used as a simple example for illustrating concepts in game theory and artificial intelligence.
  • The first player can always win or force a draw with perfect play, regardless of the opponent's moves.
  • There are 255,168 possible games of Tic Tac Toe, taking into account different move sequences and rotations/reflections of the board.
